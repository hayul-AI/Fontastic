How to Create Transparent PNG Logos

Introduction

A transparent background is essential for overlaying your generated logo onto websites, videos, or product mockups without an ugly white or black bounding box.

Step-by-step explanation

  1. Open the Fontastic Editor and enter your brand name.
  2. In the right-hand layout panel, locate the Background control.
  3. Ensure the color picker transparency channel is completely dialed down, or click the preset transparent grid icon.
  4. Click the Download PNG button.

Tips

When working with transparent backgrounds, ensure your text has enough contrast (like a white outline) to remain visible regardless of what you eventually place it over.

Best Fonts for YouTube Thumbnails

Introduction

Click-through rates (CTR) on YouTube heavily depend on how legible and striking your thumbnail text is on mobile devices.

Step-by-step explanation

  1. Choose a dense, thick (Display or Sans-Serif) font.
  2. We highly recommend checking out Bebas Neue, Anton, or Oswald.
  3. Apply a heavy drop shadow or a thick stroke outline to separate the text from the complex background image.

Tips

Keep thumbnail text to 3-5 words maximum. The bolder and tighter the lettering, the better it reads scaled down.

How to Choose the Right Logo Font

Introduction

Your font choice communicates your brand's personality before anyone even reads the word itself.

Step-by-step explanation

  1. Tech/Startups: Lean towards clean Sans-Serif options like Inter or Roboto.
  2. Luxury/Boutique: Explore elegant Serif fonts like Playfair Display or Cinzel.
  3. Creative/Friendly: Handwritten or Script fonts like Pacifico add a human touch.

Gaming Font Design Tips

Introduction

Esports teams, Twitch streamers, and game developers need aggressive, forward-leaning typography to stand out.

Tips & Examples

Browse our library for fonts like Orbitron, Righteous, or Press Start 2P. To perfect the gaming look, try combining an icy blue-to-purple gradient with a crisp white outline.

Neon Text Effects Guide

Introduction

Cyberpunk aesthetics and Synthwave art often rely on glowing neon signage effects.

Step-by-step explanation

  1. Set the editor background to pure black #000000.
  2. Choose a thin or script font like Monoton or Codystar.
  3. Set your base text color to pure white.
  4. Turn on the Neon Glow toggle, and pick a highly saturated color like hot pink #ff007f or cyan #00ffff.
