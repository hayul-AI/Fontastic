Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fontastic. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. We track basic aggregated website usage analytics (such as page views and browser types) to improve our services. We do not require registration or personal accounts.

Crucially, Fontastic does not store any user text content on our servers. Any text you type into the preview inputs or the editor is processed completely locally within your own browser. All downloaded PNG logo assets are generated client-side.

3. How We Use Information

The generic, non-identifying data we collect is used strictly to understand website traffic patterns, ensure server stability, and enhance the overall performance of the Fontastic web tool.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We use minimal cookies necessary for website functionality and anonymous analytics (e.g., Google Analytics). You can manage your cookie preferences directly through your browser settings.

5. Third Party Services

We integrate with Google Fonts to dynamically load typographic styles. These requests are subject to Google's standard privacy policies. We do not share any customized text or user interactions with third-party tracking services.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to maintain the safety of the web platform. However, no data transmission over the Internet can be unconditionally guaranteed to be 100% secure.

7. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have legal rights regarding your personal data. Given our architecture, we do not hold identifiable personal profiles to modify or delete, ensuring your inherent privacy.

8. Children's Information

Fontastic is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate modifications by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@fontastic.example.com.
